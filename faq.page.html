FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

Question 1: How can i add expenses?

Tap on the icon add (+) and tap on card name 'Expenses'. You will go to Expenses page

Question 2: Do you store all the history?

Yes. We store all your financial history until you delete them manually.

Question 3: Can I edit my expenses or income if there any changes?

Of course. You can edit your details in account, expenses, income, debt and note due to your own changes.
